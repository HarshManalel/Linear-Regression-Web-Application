Custom Dataset:
data.csv
This dataset provides a concise and descriptive representation of individuals' height and weight measurements, featuring accurate and rounded values in meters and kilograms respectively, enabling analysis of the relationship between these physical attributes.
Nutrition__Physical_Activity__and_Obesity_-_Behavioral_Risk_Factor_Surveillance_System.csv
This dataset provides comprehensive information on obesity and weight status, including years, location, data source, class, topic, question, data values, confidence limits, sample size, age, education, gender, income, race/ethnicity, geolocation, and stratification categories, enabling insights into the prevalence of obesity among adults.
placement.csv
This dataset represents students' CGPA, placement exam marks, and placement status, indicating their academic performance, exam scores, and whether they were successfully placed.
Real estate.csv
This dataset includes information on the transaction number, transaction date, house age, distance to the nearest MRT station, number of convenience stores, latitude, longitude, and the house price per unit area.
Salary_dataset.csv
This dataset contains information on years of experience and corresponding salary amounts, denoted by the columns "YearsExperience" and "Salary" respectively. It enables analysis of the relationship between work experience and salary.
Salary_Data.csv
This dataset consists of information on years of experience and corresponding salary amounts, allowing analysis of the relationship between work experience and salary.
winequality-red.csv
This dataset comprises various chemical properties of wine, including fixed acidity, volatile acidity, citric acid, residual sugar, chlorides, free sulfur dioxide, total sulfur dioxide, density, pH, sulphates, alcohol content, and quality rating. It allows for analysis and exploration of the relationships between these attributes and the quality of the wine.
